## Overview

The Analytics page provides insights into your PayGate performance, including revenue trends, transaction volumes, and product performance.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/paygate/analytics.png" alt="Analytics dashboard" />
</Frame>

## Revenue Metrics

### Total Revenue

View your total earnings in USDC:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total Revenue** | All-time earnings |
| **This Month** | Current month revenue |
| **This Week** | Current week revenue |
| **Today** | Today's revenue |

### Revenue Chart

The revenue chart displays:
- Daily, weekly, or monthly revenue
- Comparison with previous periods
- Trend indicators

## Transaction Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total Transactions** | Number of successful payments |
| **Average Transaction** | Average payment amount |
| **Success Rate** | Percentage of successful transactions |

## Product Performance

### Top Products

View your best-performing products:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Product** | Product name |
| **Sales** | Number of sales |
| **Revenue** | Total revenue from product |
| **Growth** | Change from previous period |

### Product Breakdown

- Revenue distribution by product
- Sales volume comparison
- Conversion rates

## Time Filters

Filter analytics data by:

- **Last 7 days** - Past week
- **Last 30 days** - Past month
- **Last 90 days** - Past quarter
- **This year** - Year to date
- **All time** - Complete history

## Network Filter

Analytics are calculated per network:

- **Devnet** - Test transactions (no real value)
- **Mainnet** - Real transactions

<Note>
Switch networks using the network selector to view analytics for each environment.
</Note>

## Exporting Data

Export your analytics data:

1. Select the time period
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose format (CSV or JSON)

## Key Insights

The analytics page highlights:

- **Growth trends** - Are sales increasing?
- **Peak times** - When do most purchases occur?
- **Top products** - Which products perform best?
- **Revenue distribution** - How is revenue spread across products?

<Tip>
Use analytics to identify your best-performing products and optimize your pricing strategy.
</Tip>
